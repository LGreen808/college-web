Welcome to this Rider Website! Rider University began in 1865 as Trenton Business College, created to train professionals for the rapidly changing, industrialized economy emerging after the Civil War. What started as a private business school has grown into a comprehensive university that remains driven by relevance, innovation and opportunity.
